Our Process

A streamlined 4-step approach to transform your practice with custom AI automation solutions

We analyze your current billable vs. non-billable hours.

02

Train

We teach your team how to leverage the new tools.

01

Audit

We start by conducting a comprehensive analysis of your current operations. Our team examines your workflows, identifies time-consuming manual processes, and calculates the true cost of non-billable hours. This deep dive helps us understand exactly where AI automation will deliver the most value.

  • Time tracking analysis of billable vs. non-billable hours
  • Workflow documentation and process mapping
  • Identification of automation opportunities
  • ROI projections and impact assessment
  • Current tool and system audit
02

Blueprint

Based on our audit findings, we design a custom AI architecture tailored specifically to your firm's needs. We create detailed implementation plans, select the right AI tools and platforms, and ensure seamless integration with your existing systems. This blueprint becomes your roadmap to automation success.

  • Custom AI solution architecture design
  • Tool selection and platform recommendations
  • Integration planning with existing systems
  • Detailed implementation roadmap
  • Security and compliance considerations
03

Build

Our development team brings the blueprint to life. We build custom AI agents, configure automation workflows, and integrate everything with your existing software. Throughout the build phase, we maintain strict security standards and ensure compliance with industry regulations. Your team can focus on client work while we handle the technical implementation.

  • Custom AI agent development
  • Automation workflow configuration
  • System integration and API connections
  • Security and compliance implementation
  • Testing and quality assurance
04

Train

We ensure your team is fully equipped to leverage the new AI tools. Our comprehensive training program includes hands-on sessions, detailed documentation, and ongoing support. We don't just hand over the keys—we make sure everyone knows how to use them effectively to maximize your ROI.

  • Comprehensive team training sessions
  • Detailed documentation and user guides
  • Best practices and optimization tips
  • Ongoing support and troubleshooting
  • Regular system updates and improvements
